### **III. Build Your Schedule**

*Recommendation: 2–5 hours of theory/week (early stages); 1–2 hours/month (advanced).*

**Schedule Checklist & Considerations:**

* **Theory Hours:** Schedule your desired theory hours throughout the week.  
* **Feedback Loops:** Schedule time to submit work for feedback (recommended once every few sessions).  
* **Practice Blocks:** Schedule regular dedicated practice (at least 2 hours per week).  
* **Pacing:** Avoid progressing beyond "Fundamentals 2" until at least one month of practice has been completed.  
* **Marginal Gains:** Schedule 30 minutes (1–2x per week) to review the marginal gains tracker and engage with the community.  
* **Mastery vs. Breadth:** If time is limited or an assessment is near, focus on high mastery of a few techniques rather than basic competency in many.  
* **System Reflection:** Every 2–3 weeks, write down what has changed in your learning system and what you are experimenting with.  
* **Kolb’s Cycles:** Schedule 30 minutes every 2–3 sessions to perform and submit Kolb’s cycles for feedback.
